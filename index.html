BlackJack

How to play?

  1. Click "Start Game".
  2. You'll be dealt two cards to start.
  3. The sum of the values of your cards is displayed.
  4. Points System:
    1. Numered cards have their numeric value as points.
    2. Face cards (Jack, Queen, King) each count as 10 points
    3. Ace has 11 points.
  5. Winning/Losing:
    1. If your hand value is 21, you win with Blackjack and get ₹100!
    2. If your hand value is less than 21, click new card to get as close to 21 as possible without going over.
    3. If your hand value exceeds 21, you lose the game and ₹100 are deducted.
  6. Once you're done, you can start a new game by clicking "New Game" again.
